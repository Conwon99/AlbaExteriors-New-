### Content Expansion Opportunities

#### Recommended Content Additions:

1. **Service-Specific Sections:**
   - Detailed process explanation (step-by-step)
   - Benefits of the service
   - Common problems solved
   - Equipment and techniques used
   - Maintenance tips
   - Before/after examples

2. **FAQ Sections:**
   - Common questions about each service
   - Pricing information
   - Duration of service
   - Preparation required
   - Aftercare instructions

3. **Location-Specific Content:**
   - Service areas covered
   - Local references (Paisley, Glasgow, surrounding areas)
   - Weather considerations for Scotland
   - Local regulations or permits

4. **Trust Signals:**
   - Customer testimonials
   - Case studies
   - Certifications and insurance
   - Years of experience
   - Guarantees

### Priority Recommendations

**High Priority:**
- Contact page: Add 300+ words of introductory content
- All individual service pages: Expand to match roof-cleaning page structure (1000+ words)

**Medium Priority:**
- Add FAQ sections to service pages
- Add location-specific content to all pages

**Low Priority:**
- Add blog content for long-tail keywords
- Add case studies and detailed testimonials
